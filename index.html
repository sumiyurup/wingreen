    About Us

WinGreen Ceylon

Our Fresh fruits, coconut and spices are handpicked and selected with the greatest care from our own plantations and sourced from our network of farmers in Sri Lanka and are being processed in certified factories ensuring high quality standards (GMP/ISO certified)

WinGreen Ceylon product portfolio has been identified as a premium brand in the International market for its quality & charisma.

Since we do not apply any colorings, flavors and preservatives in all our whole product assortment, we have succeeded with supplying our products with inherent pure taste, color, aroma and unchanged nutrition.
